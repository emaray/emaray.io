About Megan

I’m a Digital Scholarship and Instruction Librarian at Grinnell College. My professional interests include inclusive information literacy instruction, digital humanities, and information visualization.

Prior to my career in libraries, I worked as a freelance academic editor, assessed standardized test responses, and taught first-year composition. My academic background is in English literature, but my interests include the study of foodways and naturalist exploration of the world.

During the summer, I can be found paddling a canoe through Northwoods lakes, traipsing through prairies, and staring at trees hoping to glimpse a songbird or two. I’m also an enthusiastic home cook inclined toward ambitious canning exploits.

– April 2019
